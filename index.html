Welcome to My DevOps Journey

From Zero to CI/CD Hero - A Story of Passion, Perseverance, and Pipelines

Welcome to my portfolio! This is a space where I share the exciting journey that transformed me from a complete beginner to a skilled DevOps engineer. My story is one of determination, continuous learning, and harnessing the power of automation to build efficient CI/CD pipelines.

DevOps Highlights

  • Starting Point: In early 2024, I had no experience with cloud platforms or automation tools. My journey began with understanding the basics of AWS and version control with Git.
  • Breaking Barriers: By mid-2024, I built my first CI/CD pipeline using Jenkins and Docker, automating deployments and ensuring seamless application delivery.
  • Real-World Impact: I developed a Flask web application, integrated it with GitHub Actions, and deployed it to AWS, gaining hands-on experience with containerization and scalability.
  • Continuous Growth: Today, I focus on creating scalable solutions, managing cloud infrastructure, and building pipelines that enhance development workflows.
